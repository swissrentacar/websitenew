WEF Davos 2026 – Access Zones & Driver Regulations

Key information about hotel stickers, AA/AB access and waiting zones during the World Economic Forum Davos 2026.

Hotel Access

Hotel Access

With hotel stickers and driver badges, our chauffeurs can access AlpenGold and Belvedere Steigenberger hotels and pass the checkpoint to Promenade 101 (Migros / The Loft).

From the Migros garage, access to Hilton Garden Inn and Kongress Hotel is possible.

Red Zone

Restricted Area

The “Red Zone” (including Hertistrasse and Congress Centre area) is completely closed to all vehicles without AA / AB accreditation.

Access only for official or Forum-authorized vehicles.

AA / AB

AA / AB Accreditation

AA (government) and AB (Forum guests) stickers must be requested directly by the clients from the Forum.

Required for Hertistrasse, Kurpark, tunnel access & heliport.

Waiting Rules

Waiting Zones & Rules

Drivers must stay inside the vehicle (engine off). Entering hotels is not allowed. Pickups are only possible with precise timing coordination.

Delays may cause long reroutes due to restricted areas.
